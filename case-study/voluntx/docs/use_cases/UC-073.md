## Preconditions

- The user is authenticated as a Company (`USER.account_type = COMPANY`) and the Company is `APPROVED`.
- The user is inside the `(company)` route group at `/company/*`.

## Main Success Scenario — Company With No Subscription Views Plans

1. Company Recruiter navigates to `/company/settings/subscription` via the sidebar "Subscription" link.
2. System resolves the Company's effective subscription state by reading `COMPANY_SUBSCRIPTION` for the current `company_id`:
   - No row OR row with `status ∈ {EXPIRED, PILOT_EXPIRED}` → **no active subscription** (the Company cannot post jobs — FR-CSUB-05 / UC-077).
   - Row with `status ∈ {ACTIVE, PILOT_ACTIVE, PAST_DUE, CANCEL_PENDING}` → effective tier is the row's `tier` value, with pilot/founding flags surfaced.
3. System checks for an open Founding Partner invitation: any `FOUNDING_PARTNER_INVITATION` row for this `company_id` with `redeemed_at IS NULL` AND `revoked_at IS NULL`. If present, the invited tier and the corresponding founding `lookup_key` are surfaced (BR-073-02).
4. System loads visible pricing from the TT-024 catalog. Two cards are rendered:
   - **Hiring Partner card** — standard prices (EUR 120/mo OR EUR 1,000/yr based on toggle). Founding rate (EUR 700/yr) shown only when an HP-tier founding invitation is open. Feature list per BR-073-01.
   - **Impact Partner card** — standard price (EUR 3,600/yr; annual-only — no monthly toggle for this card). Founding rate (EUR 2,520/yr) shown only when an IP-tier founding invitation is open. **3-month Pilot** CTA (EUR 540) surfaced when the Company has no pilot history (BR-073-04). Feature list per BR-073-01.
5. If the Company has no active subscription (step 2): a top banner reminds them that job-posting (UC-022) requires an active subscription, and CTAs on both cards are "Subscribe" (→ UC-074) plus the IP card's "Start 3-month Pilot" (→ UC-075).
6. If the Company has an active subscription (step 2): the current tier card is highlighted "Current plan"; the other tier shows "Upgrade" or "Switch" (routes to UC-076 with a notice about annual-contract upgrade restrictions per BR-073-05). A "Manage Subscription" button at the top routes to UC-076.
7. If the Company has an active pilot (`status = PILOT_ACTIVE`): IP card shows "Pilot active — ends [pilot_end]. Convert to annual to retain access." with a "Convert to Annual" CTA (→ UC-075 A1 conversion flow).
8. Company Recruiter inspects the matrix and selects an action — use case ends here.

## Alternative Flows

### A1: Company With Open Founding Invitation

**Trigger:** At step 3, the Company has a non-revoked, non-redeemed FOUNDING_PARTNER_INVITATION.

**Flow:**
1. The invited tier's card displays both standard and founding rates. Example for HP: "EUR 1,000/year (standard) — or EUR 700/year (Founding Partner, 24-month locked rate)". A "Founding Partner" badge is visible on the card.
2. The "Subscribe" CTA on the invited tier auto-selects the founding `lookup_key` and includes the invite token in the request to UC-074 (BR-073-03).
3. Subscribing at the founding rate marks the FOUNDING_PARTNER_INVITATION as `redeemed_at = now` (handled in UC-074 / UC-081).
4. Founding rates are NOT shown on the non-invited tier's card.

### A2: Founding Partner Programme Closed

**Trigger:** The `founding_partner_program_open_until` PLATFORM_CONFIG value has passed; OR the 10-slot cap has been reached.

**Flow:**
1. No founding-rate UI is shown (mirrors FR-FP-01 A3).
2. Standard rates only.
3. Existing Founding Partners continue at their locked rate until `founding_lock_until` — UC-073 just doesn't surface NEW founding invitations.

### A3: Company With Active Trial — N/A

**Trigger:** N/A. Companies do not have a free trial — only NGOs do (UC-070). The Company-side equivalent is the **Impact Partner Pilot** (UC-075), which is a one-time paid product, not a free trial.

### A4: Past-Due Company Subscription

**Trigger:** At step 2, `COMPANY_SUBSCRIPTION.status = PAST_DUE`.

**Flow:**
1. Prominent banner: "Your payment failed. Update your payment method to keep your [Hiring Partner | Impact Partner] active. Without an active subscription, you cannot post new jobs."
2. CTA "Update Payment Method" → UC-076 (Customer Portal).
3. Subscribe/Pilot CTAs on other cards are NOT exposed.

### A5: Cancel-Pending Subscription

**Trigger:** `status = CANCEL_PENDING`.

**Flow:**
1. Current tier card shows "Active until [current_period_end]. Job-posting will be disabled after this date."
2. CTA "Resume Subscription" → UC-076.
3. For Founding Partners cancelling early: a warning explains the 24-month rate lock will be forfeited (BR-073-06).

### A6: Pilot About to Expire

**Trigger:** `status = PILOT_ACTIVE` AND `pilot_end - now <= 14 days`.

**Flow:**
1. Prominent banner: "Your Impact Partner pilot ends in [N days]. Convert to annual to retain access — EUR 540 pilot fee credits toward the annual price."
2. CTA "Convert to Annual" (→ UC-075 A1).

### A7: Pilot Expired Without Conversion

**Trigger:** `status = PILOT_EXPIRED`.

**Flow:**
1. IP card shows "Your pilot has ended. Subscribe to Impact Partner Annual to continue." with a "Subscribe" CTA (→ UC-074).
2. The EUR 540 credit is forfeited if more than 30 days have elapsed since `pilot_end` (BR-073-07).
3. If within the 30-day window, the IP card shows "Convert now — EUR 540 credit available until [pilot_end + 30 days]".
4. Pilot CTA is no longer shown (lifetime uniqueness — BR-073-04 already triggered).

### A8: Pricing Catalog Mismatch

Same as UC-068 A5 — graceful degradation; affected card renders without price, CTAs disabled, ops alert.

## Postconditions

### Success Postconditions

- No system state changed. UC-073 is read-only.
- The Company Recruiter has seen the available paths and can choose Subscribe / Pilot / Manage / no action.
- i18n-localized per `USER.locale`.

### Failure Postconditions

- A8: partial render; HTTP 200; ops alerted.
- DB unreachable: framework error boundary.

## Business Rules

### BR-073-01: Per-Tier Feature Display

| Section | Hiring Partner | Impact Partner |
|---|---|---|
| Unlimited job/internship postings | ✓ | ✓ |
| Walled Garden — browse and filter volunteer profiles | ✓ | ✓ |
| Walled Garden — direct contact with volunteers | — | ✓ |
| Employer verification (work email domain) | ✓ | ✓ |
| Hiring Partner badge on company profile | ✓ | ✓ |
| Quarterly Impact Report (CSRD-aligned) | — | ✓ |
| NGO partner matching (up to 2 NGOs) | — | ✓ |
| Co-branded social campaign (1/year) | — | ✓ |
| CSRD-aligned social metrics | — | ✓ |
| UN SDG mapping in quarterly report | — | ✓ |
| Email support | ✓ | ✓ |
| Quarterly account call | — | ✓ |
| Product roadmap co-creation rights | — | Founding Partners only |

Sourced from FR-CSUB-06 matrix; this UC displays it on the cards.

### BR-073-02: Founding Rates Surfaced Only To Invited Companies

Founding-rate Stripe Prices (`company_hp_annual_founding_v1`, `company_ip_annual_founding_v1`) exist in the catalog but are visible on UC-073 ONLY when the Company has a non-redeemed, non-revoked FOUNDING_PARTNER_INVITATION matching the tier. Public visitors and non-invited Companies see only standard rates. Mirrors FR-FP-01.

### BR-073-03: Founding-Rate Subscribe Carries Invite Token

When subscribing at a founding rate, the CTA passes the `invite_token` from the FOUNDING_PARTNER_INVITATION to UC-074. UC-074 validates the token, uses the founding `lookup_key`, and on successful Checkout marks the invitation as redeemed.

### BR-073-04: Pilot Lifetime Uniqueness

The 3-month Impact Partner Pilot CTA is shown iff:
- The Company has no row in COMPANY_SUBSCRIPTION with `cycle = PILOT_ONE_TIME` (any status, including PILOT_EXPIRED).
- A historical PILOT_EXPIRED row keeps the CTA hidden forever (strict lifetime uniqueness, mirrors FR-CSUB-03 BR).

### BR-073-05: No Mid-Cycle Tier Upgrade on Annual Contracts

Per FR-CSUB-04 A1: a Company on Hiring Partner ANNUAL cannot upgrade to Impact Partner ANNUAL mid-cycle through the Customer Portal. The UI explains this restriction inline; the workaround is "wait for renewal" or "cancel + start new IP subscription". HP MONTHLY subscribers can switch tiers at any time via cancel + resubscribe (UC-076 A1).

### BR-073-06: Founding Partner Cancellation Warning

When a Founding Partner views their plan during `founding_lock_until > now`, any cancellation CTA carries an explicit warning: "Cancelling forfeits your 24-month founding-rate lock. Future subscriptions will use standard rates." This is a UI hint; the actual lock forfeiture is recorded in UC-076 A2 / FR-FP-01.

### BR-073-07: Pilot Credit Has a 30-Day Conversion Window

The EUR 540 pilot fee is creditable toward the annual price IF the Company converts within 30 days of `pilot_end`. After 30 days, the credit is forfeited. UC-073 surfaces the deadline in the IP card during the conversion window.

### BR-073-08: No Free Tier — Subscribe-to-Post Discipline

Unlike NGOs, Companies have NO Free tier. The page makes this explicit when no active subscription exists: a top banner explains that posting jobs (UC-022) requires an active subscription. This is the user-visible expression of FR-CSUB-05 / UC-077.

### BR-073-09: Currency Is EUR

All Company Prices are denominated in EUR. The displayed amount comes from the TT-024 catalog at render time. The Company's locale affects only number formatting (e.g., "EUR 1,000" vs "1.000 EUR"), not currency.

### BR-073-10: All Amounts Sourced from TT-024 Catalog

No price string in templates. The catalog is the single source of truth. Price change procedure documented in TT-024 README.

### BR-073-11: Read-Only — No State Change

Same as UC-068 BR-068-09. UC-073 does not write any row; it dispatches to downstream UCs.

### BR-073-12: i18n Namespace

All on-page copy under `companysubscription.*` with `en` and `ro` translations.

## Entity Reference

**COMPANY_SUBSCRIPTION** — read-only. Effective tier, pilot state, founding flag determine card states.

**FOUNDING_PARTNER_INVITATION** — read-only. Open invitations gate the founding-rate UI.

**Catalog (TT-024)** — read-only via `resolvePriceId(lookupKey)`. Surfaces: `company_hp_monthly_v1`, `company_hp_annual_v1`, `company_hp_annual_founding_v1`, `company_ip_annual_v1`, `company_ip_annual_founding_v1`, `company_ip_pilot_v1`.

**PLATFORM_CONFIG** — `founding_partner_program_open_until` consulted to decide if A2 applies.
