Beach Cleanup — Saturday Crew

Ocean Conservation Society

Description

Join us for our weekly beach cleanup at Santa Cruz Main Beach. We provide all equipment including gloves, bags, and grabbers. Great for families and groups. No experience needed.

Requirements

  • Must be at least 16 years old
  • Able to walk on sand for 2-3 hours
  • Bring water and sunscreen
