UC-073 — View Company Subscription Plans

Company subscription plans page at /company/settings/subscription. Two side-by-side plan cards (Hiring Partner / Impact Partner), HP-local monthly↔annual toggle, conditional founding-rate surfacing, conditional 3-month Impact Partner pilot CTA, lifecycle banners (past-due, cancel-pending, pilot-expiring), and a Manage Subscription entry to UC-076. Read-only (BR-073-11); all CTAs route to UC-074 / UC-075 / UC-076. Prices read from TT-024 catalog at render time (BR-073-10).

Layout rule (DESIGN_RULES §1 — Company group): This page lives in the (company) route group. Currency (BR-073-09): All Company Prices are EUR. We render "EUR 1,000", not "€1,000" or "1.000 EUR" — matches the wireframe.

i18n (BR-073-12): All copy lives under companysubscription.*. Both ro and en required at delivery. RO is the default reflected below; toggle the chrome to compare.

Accessibility & Interactions WCAG AA
Keyboard:
  • HP cycle toggle: role=tablist · aria-selected on each tab · arrow-left/right cycles between Monthly and Annual within the HP card

  • Pilot-active progress bar: role=progressbar with aria-valuenow, aria-valuemin, aria-valuemax, aria-label
  • No modals in UC-073 — Escape not bound
Contrast:
  • forest-900 on cream-50 = 12.1:1 (AAA)
  • coral-600 on coral-50/cream-100/gold-50 gradient ≥ 4.8:1 (AA — sampled at darkest gradient midpoint)
  • gold-900 on gold-50 (founding pill) = 9.4:1 (AAA)
  • red-800 on red-50 (past-due banner) = 8.6:1 (AAA)
  • amber-900 on amber-50 (cancel-pending / pilot-expiring) = 8.0:1 (AAA)
  • White on forest-700 (HP CTA) = 6.2:1 · White on coral-500 (IP CTA) = 5.1:1 · White on gold gradient (founding CTA) ≥ 4.7:1 — all AA for normal text
Reduced motion: @media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce) disables price-anim, fadeUp, shimmer, and the wg-shimmer-overlay gradient sweep on the IP card.
Interactions:
  • Card hover: 4 px lift + soft forest-shadow on .plan-card:hover (suppressed on disabled / pilot-grayed cards)
  • HP cycle toggle: Click swaps active tab; the HP card price fades-in via price-anim keyframes (0.35 s, ease) and the savings pill highlight appears when Annual is selected
  • CTA press: active:scale-[0.98] instant feedback (DESIGN_RULES §6)
  • Founding badge: static + decorative; the gold gradient on the founding CTA carries the affordance (no tooltip — explanation lives in the founding pill row below the price)
  • Pilot-active countdown: static snapshot (RSC) — the progress bar is computed at render time from (now - pilot_start) / (pilot_end - pilot_start)
  • State swap (designer chrome only): instant DOM update; the live page never swaps states client-side — each state corresponds to a different server render
