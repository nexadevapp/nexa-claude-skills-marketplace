UC-001 — Register

Account registration flow for new users. Supports email signup and social OAuth (Google, LinkedIn).

Actor: Visitor (Unauthenticated) Route: /register Priority: Critical
1. Account Type Selection
Interaction: User selects one of three account types before proceeding to the registration form. The selected card gets a blue border and checkmark. This determines the role field in the user record.

2. Social Login Buttons
Interaction: OAuth buttons redirect to Google/LinkedIn consent screens. On return, the user is auto-registered and redirected to onboarding. If a matching account exists, it merges or shows an error.

4. Password Strength Meter States
Strength logic: Weak = <6 chars or common pattern. Medium = 8+ chars, mixed case. Strong = 12+ chars, mixed case + numbers + symbols. The meter fills progressively with color feedback.

6. Successful Registration
After success: User sees a confirmation screen. For email registration, they must verify their email. For OAuth, they are redirected directly to onboarding.
