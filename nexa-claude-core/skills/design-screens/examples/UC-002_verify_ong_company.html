4. Approve Confirmation Dialog
Success Flow

Approve Organization

You are about to approve EcoWild Foundation (CUI: RO12345678). The organization will receive email notification and gain access to the platform.

Interaction: On "Confirm Approval", the system sends a welcome email to the organization, sets verification_status = 'approved', and logs the action in the admin audit trail. The admin notes are stored for reference.
5. Reject with Reason Dialog
Rejection Flow

Reject Organization

You are rejecting EcoWild Foundation. The organization will be notified with the reason below.

Interaction: A rejection reason must be selected from the dropdown. The "Other" option enables the custom reason field as required. On "Confirm Rejection", a rejection notification email is sent to the organization with the selected reason and additional details. Status is set to rejected.
